## 7. PHARMACY & COMPOUNDING

### Current Site Claims
- "Licensed Pharmacy Facilities"
- "All medication compounded in licensed, state-licensed US pharmacies"

### Research Findings

**FDA Registration:**
- Compounding facilities can register as "outsourcing facilities" under Section 503B
- OR operate under Section 503A (state-licensed pharmacies)
- Outsourcing facilities must:
  - Register annually (Oct 1-Dec 31)
  - Pay annual establishment fee
  - Comply with CGMP requirements
  - Subject to FDA inspection

**State Licensing:**
- States regulate compounding through licensure
- State-licensed pharmacies must comply with state requirements

**Action Required:**
- ‚ö†Ô∏è **VERIFY:**
  - Pharmacy partners are state-licensed
  - If outsourcing facility, verify FDA registration
  - Verify USP 797/800 compliance
  - Document pharmacy licensing status
- Update claims to be more specific: "State-licensed compounding pharmacies" or "FDA-registered outsourcing facilities"
