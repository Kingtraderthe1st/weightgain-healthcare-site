Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

WeightGain ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address
  • Date of birth and biological sex
  • Payment information (processed securely by Stripe)
  • Health information related to lab tests and hormone optimization

Health Information (PHI)

As a healthcare service provider, we collect Protected Health Information (PHI) including:

  • Lab test results and biomarker data
  • Medical history relevant to hormone optimization
  • Treatment protocols and medication information

3. HIPAA Compliance

WeightGain is committed to complying with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). We implement administrative, physical, and technical safeguards to protect your Protected Health Information (PHI).

  • All PHI is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access to PHI is limited to authorized personnel only
  • We maintain audit logs of all PHI access
  • Staff receive regular HIPAA training

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our hormone optimization services
  • Process lab test orders and deliver results
  • Communicate with you about your account and services
  • Send service-related notifications and updates
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Understand how you use our website
  • Improve our services and user experience

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some website functionality.

6. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Healthcare Providers: Licensed physicians who review your results and prescribe treatments
  • Laboratory Partners: CLIA-certified labs that process your blood tests
  • Payment Processors: Stripe for secure payment processing
  • Service Providers: Companies that help us operate our business

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • 256-bit SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Encrypted database storage
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Multi-factor authentication for staff access

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal and health information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Receive a copy of your health records
  • Opt out of marketing communications

9. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Health records are retained in accordance with applicable state and federal laws.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@weightgain.com
  • Phone: 1-800-WEIGHTGAIN
  • Mail: WeightGain, Privacy Officer, [Address]

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
