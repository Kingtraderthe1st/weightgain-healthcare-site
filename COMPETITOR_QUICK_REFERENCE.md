## Top 5 Customer Pain Points (Your Opportunities)

### 1. Hidden Fees & Pricing Confusion ⚠️ VERY HIGH
- **Problem:** Membership + medication costs separate
- **Example:** Hone: $129 + $60 = $189 (but advertised as $129)
- **Your Advantage:** $249 all-inclusive, no surprises
- **Action:** Emphasize "Everything Included" vs competitors

### 2. Cancellation & Refund Issues ⚠️ VERY HIGH
- **Problem:** FTC investigating, difficult cancellations
- **Example:** Hims & Hers under FTC investigation
- **Your Advantage:** Cancel Anytime + 90-day guarantee
- **Action:** Make guarantee prominent on homepage

### 3. Customer Service Gaps ⚠️ HIGH
- **Problem:** Slow responses, limited channels
- **Example:** Hone: 107 BBB complaints, 78 in last year
- **Your Advantage:** Fast email support + multiple channels
- **Action:** Set 4-hour response SLA, train staff

### 4. Shipping & Lab Delays ⚠️ HIGH
- **Problem:** Slow processing, delivery issues
- **Example:** Hone: 9 delivery complaints, lab delays
- **Your Advantage:** Ships same day, 4,500+ lab locations
- **Action:** Emphasize speed and coverage

### 5. Limited Treatment Options ⚠️ MODERATE
- **Problem:** TRT only, limited formats
- **Example:** Fountain: Topical only, AlphaMD: No troches/gel
- **Your Advantage:** TRT + HGH, multiple formats, personalized
- **Action:** Highlight comprehensive protocol options

1. **True All-Inclusive Pricing**

2. **Faster Time-to-Treatment**
