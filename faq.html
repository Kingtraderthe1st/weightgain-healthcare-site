Getting Started

How does WeightGain work?

WeightGain is a comprehensive hormone optimization program that works in four simple steps:

  1. Order your lab kit - Choose your membership and we'll send you a lab requisition.
  2. Get tested - Visit any Quest Diagnostics location for a simple blood draw.
  3. Receive your protocol - Our physicians analyze 50+ biomarkers and create your personalized plan.
  4. Optimize and monitor - Follow your protocol with quarterly check-ins to track progress.
Who is WeightGain for?

WeightGain is designed for adults (18+) who want to optimize their hormones for better energy, strength, mental clarity, and overall performance. Common reasons people join include:

  • Feeling tired or low energy despite adequate sleep
  • Difficulty building muscle or losing fat
  • Decreased motivation or mental sharpness
  • Interest in proactive health optimization
  • Athletes looking for legal performance enhancement
What states do you serve?

We currently serve most U.S. states. Due to state regulations on direct-to-consumer lab testing, we cannot serve residents of New York, New Jersey, Hawaii, or Rhode Island at this time. We're working to expand availability.

Lab Testing

What biomarkers do you test?

Our comprehensive panel includes 50+ biomarkers covering:

  • Hormones: Testosterone (Total & Free), Estradiol, DHEA-S, Cortisol, Thyroid (TSH, T3, T4), and more
  • Metabolic: Fasting Glucose, HbA1c, Insulin, Lipid Panel
  • Inflammation: hsCRP, Homocysteine
  • Vitamins & Minerals: Vitamin D, B12, Ferritin, Magnesium
  • Organ Function: Liver enzymes, Kidney function, CBC
Do I need to fast before my blood draw?

Yes, we recommend fasting for 8-12 hours before your blood draw for the most accurate results. Water is allowed and encouraged. Schedule your appointment for the morning if possible.

How long until I get my results?

Lab results typically arrive within 3-5 business days after your blood draw. Our medical team will review your results and you'll receive your personalized protocol within 2-3 business days after that.

Where do I go for my blood draw?

We partner with Quest Diagnostics and LabCorp, with 4,500+ locations nationwide. After ordering, you'll receive a lab requisition you can take to any partner location. No appointment is usually necessary.

Treatment & Protocol

What treatments do you offer?

Based on your lab results and health goals, our physicians may recommend:

  • Testosterone Replacement Therapy (TRT)
  • Growth Hormone Peptides
  • Thyroid Optimization
  • DHEA Supplementation
  • Vitamin/Mineral Protocols
  • Lifestyle and nutrition guidance

All treatments are FDA-approved and prescribed by licensed physicians based on your specific needs.

Is hormone therapy safe?

When properly monitored by qualified physicians, hormone optimization may be safe and effective for many individuals. We use bioidentical hormones at physiological doses and monitor your levels quarterly. Our protocol recommendations follow evidence-based guidelines from endocrinology societies. However, individual results and safety profiles vary, and hormone therapy is not appropriate for everyone. Always consult with your physician about your specific health situation.

How are medications delivered?

All medications are shipped directly to your door in discreet packaging. We use temperature-controlled shipping when necessary to maintain medication integrity. Most orders arrive within 3-5 business days.
